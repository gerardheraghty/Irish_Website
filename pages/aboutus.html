As a language teacher of German and Irish, I found that there was a considerable difference in the availability of German and Irish language resources online. While there was German language resources easily available online, I had to spend a lot of my preparation time, creating my own Irish language learning resources for my classroom. This was very time-consuming and it was difficult to create high-quality interactive resources for every class/topic. I came up with the idea for scoláire.ie by watching YouTube language channels and researching language learning resources online. The students in the videos are Irish learners and make the videos more relatable and give examples of real-life conversations. The aim of this website is to encourage more people to learn and revisit their Irish and to provide useful content for schools.
